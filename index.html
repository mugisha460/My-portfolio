About Me

I’m a Financial and Health Literacy Coach with over 5 years of experience empowering communities across Uganda and East Africa. My work focuses on bridging the gap between financial stability and personal well-being by delivering practical, culturally relevant education. Through coaching, workshops, and digital platforms, I help individuals, youth, and families make informed choices about money management, disease prevention, and healthy living. My mission is to equip people with the knowledge and tools they need to achieve long-term wellness and build resilient, financially empowered communities.

My Projects

Legacy and Generational Wealth Creation

The Legacy and Generational Wealth Creation Project is a transformative initiative designed to empower individuals and families with the tools, mindset, and strategies needed to build lasting financial legacies. Through a blend of financial literacy coaching, estate planning education, investment guidance, and values-based legacy planning, we help communities take control of their financial future. Our goal is to shift mindsets from short-term survival to long-term wealth sustainability — creating a ripple effect of prosperity across generations. Whether you're just starting your financial journey or planning how to pass on your wealth, this project equips you with the knowledge to make it happen with intention and impact.

Health Literacy for Business Startups & Owners project

The Health Literacy for Business Startups & Owners Project is designed to equip entrepreneurs with the knowledge and tools to prioritize their well-being as a core part of building and sustaining a successful business. Recognizing that a founder’s physical and mental health directly impacts productivity, decision-making, and long-term growth, this project provides practical guidance on managing stress, preventing burnout, and creating health-conscious workplaces. By integrating health into their business strategy, entrepreneurs can lead with clarity, resilience, and purpose — building not just profitable ventures, but sustainable ones.
